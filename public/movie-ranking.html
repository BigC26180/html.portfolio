The Best Movies According to Colin

My Top Three Movies of All Time

Gladiator

A film starring Russell Crowe who is a Roman General, who becomes a slave, who then becomes a gladiator in the Collesium of Rome, with the aim of bringing down a vendictive Roman Emperor who killed his wife and child.

Top Gun

80's movies starring Tom Cruise as Pete "Maverick" Mitchell who gets sent to the Naval Fighter Pilot School to see who is the best of the best.

The pursuit of Happiness

A feel good film starring Will Smith as a man at rock bottom who has a chance meeting with a stock broker, who changes his mindset to become a very rich man.
